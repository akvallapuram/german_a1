### Exam Structure Reminder (Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1)

> **Official practice exam (Modellsatz):** [https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/](https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/) — interactive online version with all 4 modules

| Module | Duration | Parts | What to Expect (verified from [official Modellsatz](https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/)) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **[Hören](https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/hoeren.php)** (Listening) | ~20 min | 3 parts | **Teil 1** (6 items): Short everyday dialogues — pick the right picture (prices, times, food, directions). Heard **twice**. **Teil 2** (4 items): Public announcements (airport, train, shop) — true/false. Heard **once**. **Teil 3** (5 items): Short phone conversations — write down info (phone numbers, meeting places, days, items). Heard **twice**. |
| **[Lesen](https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/lesen.php)** (Reading) | ~25 min | 3 parts | **Teil 1** (5 items): Read 2 short personal messages (notes, emails) — true/false. **Teil 2** (5 items): Read 2 internet/ad texts per question — pick which one has the info you need. **Teil 3** (5 items): Read short signs/notices (post office hours, restaurant signs, station rules) — true/false. |
| **[Schreiben](https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/schreiben.php)** (Writing) | ~20 min | 2 parts | **Teil 1** (5 items): Fill in a form with missing info (names, dates, numbers, addresses, payment method). **Teil 2** (1 item): Write a short message/email (~30 words) with Anrede + Gruß, responding to 3 content prompts (e.g., reason for writing, a request, a question). Scoring: 3 pts per content point fulfilled + 1 pt for appropriate text format. |
| **[Sprechen](https://bfu.goethe.de/a1_sd1/sprechen.php)** (Speaking) | ~15 min | 3 parts | **Teil 1** (~5 min): Self-introduction — Name, Alter, Land, Wohnort, Sprachen, Beruf, Hobby. **Teil 2** (~5 min): Draw word cards from a theme (e.g., *Essen und Trinken*, *Einkaufen*) — ask a question AND give an answer about each word. **Teil 3** (~5 min): See picture cards of objects/situations — formulate a request (*Können Sie mir bitte...?*) and respond to others' requests. |
